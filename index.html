About Me

I'm a self taught frontend developer based in Liverpool. I studied pharmaceutical sciences at John Moore's uni and followed that up by going into teaching.

A few years ago I picked up web development as a hobby and it's fully taken over my life, much to the disdain of my partner.

I specialise in ReactJS which implies (rightfully) some degree of mastery over CSS, HTML, and of course JavaScript.
